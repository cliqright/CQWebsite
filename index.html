FAQ

What is CliqRight App?

A community sharing mobile app to share and borrow things with your neighbours, friends and beyond. It makes sharing and managing your items easy.

How do I setup this App?

Download the app from App Store by following link on this website or by searching "CliqRight" in the App Store. Signup and create a profile by providing some basic information. And your are ready to make most of our app.

Does it cost anything to become a member?

No, all it takes is willingness to borrow and share. We dont charge you a penny. Mobile provider might charge you for data or messaging.

What is your policy regarding privacy?

We take your privacy very seriously. You can get details by reading our privacy policy here.

Are there more help resources available?

You can contact us through the details provided in the contact us section. We will do our best to provide needed help.

What are groups?

Groups on our app lets friends, acquaintances, or people with similar interests to share items though our app.

How to join groups?

You can look for groups near you and read description of group and items listed in the group. You can request join any group you are intreseted in joining. Group Owner or administrator of group will review your request and accept you into group.

Is it required to create new group?

No, you are not required to create new group. You can create one if you want to share with only closed group of friends or neighbours.

Who can join my groups?

You can invite any contact in your phone to join your group. once they accept your invite will be made part of your group. Any app user with in 100 miles of your location can see your group, read description and request to join your group. You can review all requests to join the group and decide to accept or decline the request.

Do i need to create a groups?

No, you dont have to create new group unless you want to share only with in your closed group.

How to list items?

You can click the camera icon in menu which will let you provide a name and description for your item. You can also add photos of your item. You can share the item as public item or share only in specific groups you are part of.
